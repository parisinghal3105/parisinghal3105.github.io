On Discipline and Becoming

April 2026 • reflective essay • personal archive

Hello.

My name is Pari Singhal! Welcome to my first blog! At the time of writing, I am 12 years old and will turn 13 soon on May 31st.

Discipline as Self-Respect

I created this website because of my passion for literature and the art of devoting myself to writing. Here, you will find hopefully dozens of blogs, each one exploring topics that pique my interest.

I hope my future blogs inspire you to create something similar, as passion should be shared instead of kept to oneself.

“If you want to become someone, behave like her before you feel like her.”

This blog will be the start of something wonderful - I promise.

If I can keep one vow today, even a small one, I will be closer to the person I am trying to become. Not in a dramatic way. In a quiet way. In a way that lasts.

As Millicent Fawcett and the galvanzing Suffragists once believed in:

"Courage calls to courage everywhere.

I hope this page inspires you to be creative
